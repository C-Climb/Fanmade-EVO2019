What is EVO?

The Evo Championship Series (commonly referred to as EVO) is a fighting game event held in Las Vegas, Nevada. The event offers tournaments for various video games, including Street Fighter V, Tekken 7, and Dragon Ball FighterZ. EVO 2018 had over 10,000 participants, remaining one of the largest fighting game events! Learn More
